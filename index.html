Yes, ForMenIndiaHelp is 100% free, open-source, and contains no ads or subscription fees. It is built to serve men in legal distress with educational rights resources.

The app covers Indian law guides including Section 498A IPC (Cruelty), CrPC 125 (Maintenance), DV Act (Domestic Violence), Child Custody, Divorce, and Anticipatory Bail procedures.
