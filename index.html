Published Features (v1.4)

Powerful Tools Right in Your Pocket

Designed specifically to educate, protect, and support men in distress.

Offline Legal Knowledge Base

Read extensive guides on sections like 498A IPC, CrPC 125, DV Act, and Child Custody without needing an active internet connection.

AI Emotional Counselling Bot

Powered by Google Gemini. Tailored mental health support utilizing evidence-based CBT, ACT, and DBT TIPP crisis frameworks.

In-App eCourts Portal & CNR Assistant

Directly access the official Indian Judiciary portal with a 1-Click Smart CNR Copy Assistant powered by expo-clipboard.

IndianKanoon Live Search & Modal

Search verified Supreme Court and High Court judgments in real-time with clean, native in-app reading modals.

Multi-lingual Audio Guides

Listen to legal summaries and step-by-step procedures in English, Hindi, and regional languages while on the go.

1-Click API Onboarding

Hassle-free setup for Google AI Studio and IndianKanoon API tokens with visual 3-step configuration guides.
