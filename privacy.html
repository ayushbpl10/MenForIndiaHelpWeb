Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 2026

ForMenIndiaHelp ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Data Collection

We prioritize your privacy. We do not collect, store, or share your personal data on any external servers.

  • API Keys: Any API keys (e.g., Gemini AI keys) you enter are stored locally and securely on your device. They are never transmitted to our servers.
  • Chat History: Conversations with the AI assistant are processed directly between your device and the AI provider (Google). We do not record, monitor, or store these conversations.

2. Third-Party Services

Our app integrates with third-party services which have their own privacy policies:

  • Google Generative AI (Gemini): Used for the AI assistant.
  • YouTube: Used for streaming video documentaries.

3. Permissions

To enable the Emergency SOS feature, the app requests Location Access (GPS) to generate your current coordinates, which are only sent directly via SMS to your own chosen trusted contacts. This location data is processed locally on your device, and is never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any third-party servers.
